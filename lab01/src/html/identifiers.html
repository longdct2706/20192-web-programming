5 Kinds of Identifiers

Citizen Identification Card Number

Citizen Identification Card number is deemed as the personal identification number. An example of this kind of identifier is 013599999 (old type) or 001183000001 (new type). A citizen identification card includes the followings:

  • The national emblem of Vietnam; printed texts such as the sentence Socialist Republic of Vietnam, Independence - Freedom - Happiness , and the card title Citizen Identification Card; the cardholder's photo, card number, surname, middle name and first name provided in the birth certificate, birth date, gender, nationality, place of origin, permanent residence; expiry date, must be printed at the front side of each card.
  • Encrypted information section; finger print, the cardholder’s anthropometric identities; date of issue; surname, middle name and first name, designation, signature of the issuer and the card issuing authority’s stamp with the national emblem, must be printed at the back side of each card.

Passport Number

Vietnamese passports are issued to citizens of Vietnam to facilitate international travel.

A current Vietnamese passport contains 48 pages and 2 bright green cover pages. An example of this kind of identifier is B3467031. On the back of the front cover, there is a note from Vietnamese Government addressed to the authorities of all other states as follows.

This passport remains the property of Socialist Republic of Vietnam and is issued to a Vietnamese citizen only. This passport is valid for all countries unless otherwise endorsed. The Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam requests all competent authorities concerned to allow the bearer of this passport to pass freely and to afford him/her such assistance and protection as may be necessary.

Student ID Number

Each student is provided with a student card or a student certificate including a corresponding student ID number. An example of this kind of identifier is 20770001. The student ID number help differentiate students in the same college or university while the card or the certificate allows certifying that he/she is a student of a college or a university.

Social Insurance Number

An example of this kind of identifier is 0120777734. Social insurance is any government-sponsored program with the following four characteristics:

  • the benefits, eligibility requirements and other aspects of the program are defined by statute;
  • explicit provision is made to account for the income and expenses (often through a trust fund);
  • it is funded by taxes or premiums paid by (or on behalf of) participants (but additional sources of funding may be provided as well);
  • and the program serves a defined population, and participation is either compulsory or so heavily subsidized that most eligible individuals choose to participate.
Social insurance has also been defined as a program whose risks are transferred to and pooled by an often government organization legally required to provide certain benefits

Individual tax code

Any individual present in Vietnam who has taxable income must obtain an individual tax code. Those who have taxable employment income must submit the tax registration file to their employer; the employer will subsequently submit this to the local tax office. For individuals with taxable non-employment income, they must submit their tax registration file directly to the district tax office. An example of this kind of identifier is 0102859048.
